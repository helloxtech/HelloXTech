Rental Smart Platform

Property management and rental operations in one BC-focused system.

Rental Smart helps landlords attract qualified renters and helps residents manage tenancy tasks through one connected experience. From listings and leasing to rent workflows and maintenance updates, your team stays aligned end-to-end.

Features

Designed for real property workflows, not generic CRM screens.

Verified listing management

Create listing pages with media, availability, and screening-ready details for BC renters.

Application and inquiry capture

Track interested renters, review applications, and route follow-up tasks through one queue.

Lease lifecycle tracking

Manage move-ins, renewals, recurring charges, and tenancy milestones from one dashboard.

Rent workflow visibility

Monitor billed, paid, and overdue balances with portfolio-level and unit-level drilldown.

Maintenance operations

Receive requests, track status updates, and maintain service history for each property.

Role-based experience
